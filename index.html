A model for the local community

Green spaces

Design parks, community gardens and tree-lined streets to reduce heat, improve air quality, and create places for everyone to relax.

Smart transport

Include bike lanes, pedestrian routes, bus stops and smart traffic planning so people can move easily without relying on cars.

Essential infrastructure

Plan schools, healthcare, water, and energy systems that support a growing community and make the city more resilient.
