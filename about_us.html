What this project is about

This Year 8 project explores how our local community can become more sustainable by using a model city map to show green spaces, transport, energy, and services.

Why a model city map?

A map helps everyone understand how parks, bike lanes, schools, and smart infrastructure work together to create a healthier city.

Our community focus

The plan is designed for local residents, schools, and families who want a greener, safer, and smarter neighbourhood.
