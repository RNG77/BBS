Key ideas for a livable smart city

These core features explain how green space, transport, infrastructure, and community services can support a more sustainable neighbourhood.

Green spaces

Parks, gardens, and trees make the city cooler, cleaner, and more welcoming for everyone.

Smart transport

Paths for walking, cycling, and public transport help people move around safely and reduce car use.

Essential services

Schools, health facilities, water, and energy systems are located so they are easy for the community to reach.

Community design

Shared spaces and smart tools help residents feel connected and supported in their neighbourhood.
